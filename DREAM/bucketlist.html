Bucket List

Lifetime List of Buckets

A single timeline of experiences, goals, and achievements I wish to attain.

In Progress

To be Financially Stable

growing up as a kid who wasn't really given much money, although still grateful for what I have, I want to be able to provide for myself without worrying about money. I want to be able to save up, invest, and to spend, obviously. I want to be able to travel, buy things I want, and not have to worry about bills or emergencies. I want to be able to give back to my community and help others who are in need, Mainly just friends who have helped me through the years.

Dreaming

The Dream of Being a Musician

My Family, especially my father, was unfortunately too pessimistic. He always tell me being a musician isn't a career, not a job, so I won't live from it. I resisted that kind of mindset, refusing to be held back by people who didn't "Risk". That's why, no matter how long it takes, I will keep chasing this dream. Having to let go of my dream of being a volleyball player really hurt, it was like he was right. So know I will be better, and prove him wrong. I want to be able to make music that people can relate to, and that can help them through their own struggles. I want to be able to perform on stage and connect with my audience through my music. I want to be able to collaborate with other musicians and create something truly special. I want to be able to share my music with the world and make a positive impact on people's lives.

Travel

Explore the World

I want to see what the world has to offer. I was a homebody, my room being my safe place, even at times it isn't. I don't have a definite place to belong. I want to go anywhere, and everywhere. Any place that I can enjoy, I will consider my home. Don't get me wrong, I love my home, however the environment was unhealthy. Pessimists, Narcissists, Hypocrites, and overall, Toxic people. I want to be able to escape that, and find a place where I can be happy and healthy. I'm honestly tired of getting dragged down into bad energy when I should bring myself up to carry on with my day. It's like there are weights on my chest everytime I'm inside my own home. But when I go outside, Seeing other people just be happy, and be happy because of me, lightens my heart. That's why I want to be out, I want to get out. I'm happy outside.
