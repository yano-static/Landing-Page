Travel Goals

Dream Places

🇫🇷 Paris, France

I want to experience the romantic atmosphere, world-class art museums like the Louvre, and iconic architecture like the Eiffel Tower.

🇯🇵 Tokyo, Japan

A mix of traditional culture and futuristic technology. I look forward to cherry blossoms, anime culture, and the food scene.

🇺🇸 New York City, USA

Visiting for the skyscrapers, Central Park, and the high-energy vibe of Broadway and Manhattan.

🇬🇷 Santorini, Greece

Drawn by the famous sunsets, the stunning whitewashed buildings, and the view over the Aegean Sea.

🇮🇩 Bali, Indonesia

A place for relaxation, spiritual temple visits, and enjoying the beautiful tropical beaches.
