Miyagi, Japan

As a dedicated volleyball fan, visiting Miyagi Prefecture is more than a trip—it's a pilgrimage. Home to the iconic Sendai Gymnasium, this region bridges the gap between the high-energy world of anime and the rich, real-world Japanese volleyball culture.

The Fan's Itinerary

  • Visit the legendary Sendai City Gymnasium
  • Immerse in the local volleyball culture
  • Explore the scenic landscapes of the Miyagi Prefecture
  • Taste authentic local cuisine like Zunda Sago
