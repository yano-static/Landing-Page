TV Shows

These are collections of where my free time cycles, Especially Rank 1, which I have watched 14..ish times already. Shows that focuses mainly on good vibes, motivation, and morals.

Sports · Shonen

Haikyuu!!

★★★★★

Directed by: Susumu Mitsunaka

Stars: Ayumu Murase, Kaito Ishikawa

A heart-pumping journey of Shoyo Hinata, who despite his short stature, aspires to become a volleyball legend. It’s a masterclass in character development and the beauty of team sports.

Drama · Music

Forest of Piano

★★★★★

Directed by: Gaku Nakatani

Stars: Soma Saito, Natsuki Hanae

Two boys from vastly different backgrounds find common ground through their love for a mysterious piano found in the middle of a forest. A visual and auditory feast for music lovers.

Seinen · Art

Blue Period

★★★★★

Directed by: Koji Masunari

Stars: Hiromu Mineta, Yumiri Hanamori

The agonizing yet beautiful struggle of Yatora Yaguchi as he discovers the joy of painting and strives to get into the most prestigious art university in Japan.
