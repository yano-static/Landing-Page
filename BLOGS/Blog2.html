How I caught a burnout
What? * How? * Why?

What I Caught?

January 29, 2026 · by Yano Palanas

As you grow older, The more you do things, The more you spend time on it, you grow more and more tiresome of them, making you prone to Burnout . It’s something that you won’t be able to avoid, unless you can dodge a bullet. Now that you can’t avoid it, you can only cope, or do your best to deal with it. But why is burnout so inevitable?

How I caught?

Living in a toxic household, where emotions are kept, apologies are turned into favors, where grades meant everything. This type of environment has been building my personality: from bursting into breakdowns because of bottled emotions, to escaping every problem I can’t handle by avoiding it. Every child has experienced this type of growth; however, it can be different for each one.

For me, every factor of that—and if you include problems from outside your home—you are only human to get tired of it. You get tired of hiding everything you feel, grow tired of having to live up to their expectations. As I grow tired, I try lessening their expectations. As much as I want to make people proud, I can’t make everyone be proud of me. I can only be so much, but I can be more if I’m allowed to grow on my own.

Why I caught?

Now that the bad things went by, now that you’ve been through a lot, it will make you stronger. It will help you deal with things a lot better, be more patient, and be an overall better person— as long as you take care of yourself. You’ll be fine.
